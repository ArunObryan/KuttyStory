Chapter One

The Moment

One day he was sitting on a bench in a quiet park. There was a lake in front of him, surrounded by lots of Sakura trees. It was spring season and the trees were filled with pink flowers. A cold breeze blew from his right to left. It was a shower of pink flowers on him—calm, happy, like a blessing.

He heard a giggle of a child, quite some distance to his right. He turned and saw two kids enjoying this shower of flowers. It was a wholesome moment. As the kids were jumping and shouting, one man gave his hand to the short cute one. The little one jumped and grabbed his giant index finger, which fit into her whole palm.

The man was in a full black-grey long coat, well dressed and groomed. He looked fit, in his mid-thirties, with broad shoulders and a muscular lean body. He looked down to his left and started moving forward. The big one took the hand of a lady in a beige coat with a hat—a classy lady with a big bright smile. She looked down, talking to her big boy kid who wanted to play there a while longer. It felt like they were getting late for somewhere and wanted to go fast. So all four started walking together, with the breeze showering flowers on them. A dream moment for the man sitting there, observing this beautiful family.

He started thinking: if there was a chance to have this kind of moment in his life, with whom would it be? The question arose in his mind. On the other half of his brain, the answer was already there. It didn't take much time for the face to come to his mind. Memories started flooding his brain—Vaan ingae neelam angae Intha uvamaiku iruvarum Vilakamantheno …ooo…ooo..

Stages of Life

In my journey I have seen different phases of love and life. Each one demands different kinds of love. It starts from being one's best friend, lover, partner, parents, grandparents... It goes on. Each phase demands more effort than the previous one. Each phase has its own charm where our love is shown in different actions.

During the dating phase we spend time together, roam around, travel, party with friends, talk for hours—this feels like love. During the marriage phase, how one looks after each other's family, respects every person, and makes space for them in their life—even when their partner is not available. It feels like the love has extended to the family members as well. It grows bigger and is shown in the responsibility they take for each other's family.

During the motherhood phase, the man might show his love by massaging her feet, handling her mood swings, spending time with her to carry the child into this world.
